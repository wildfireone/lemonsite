LEMON:
Learning Mobile on the go.

Creating a Mobile Learning Pedagogical Approach Supported by a Mobile Toolkit for Flexible Learning Opportunities in Joint International Projects

An international Alliance of four Higher Education Institutes (HEI) and four businesses will approach the aims and objectives of modernizing and internationalizing education and training in the project LEMON, by jointly developing a new multidisciplinary innovative pedagogical mLearning model with key stakeholders (educational institutes, teachers and students’ associations and businesses) and a toolkit for the model to be implemented in. 

Learn More

LEMON is internationalizing and modernizing education and training in the EU.

The objective of the Alliance is to design and co-create a comprehensive pedagogical mLearning model and tools for students and staff of HEIs and staff and clients of business partners to carry out studies and training in interdisciplinary international projects together with business life. Interactive, international personalized learning makes competence-based studying relevant and the ways to learn modern.
